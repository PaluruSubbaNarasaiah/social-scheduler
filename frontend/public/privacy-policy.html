Privacy Policy for Simple Social Scheduler

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password
  • Social Media Data: Access tokens for connected platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn)
  • Content Data: Posts, images, and scheduling information you create
  • Usage Data: Analytics and app usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide social media scheduling services
  • To post content to your connected social media accounts
  • To provide analytics and insights
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To improve our services

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • With social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn) to post your content
  • With payment processors (Razorpay) for subscription management
  • When required by law

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure token storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request data deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent

7. Facebook Data Use

For Facebook integration, we:

  • Only access data necessary for posting
  • Do not store Facebook content longer than necessary
  • Comply with Facebook Platform Policy
  • Provide data deletion upon request

8. Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Email us at privacy@simplesocialscheduler.com
  • Use the data deletion form below

9. Contact Us

For privacy concerns or data deletion requests:

10. Updates

We may update this policy. Users will be notified of significant changes via email.

This privacy policy complies with GDPR, CCPA, and Facebook Platform Policy requirements.
